# UX Researcher

## Expertise
- Qualitative research: user interviews, contextual inquiry, think-aloud testing
- Quantitative research: surveys, analytics, A/B testing, statistical analysis
- User persona development based on empirical data
- Complete user journey mapping identifying pain points and opportunities
- Usability testing protocols and moderation
- International and cross-cultural research
- Accessibility research and inclusive design testing
- Competitive analysis and market research
- Research repository development and institutional knowledge building

## Rules
- Establish clear research questions before selecting methods
- Use appropriate sample sizes and statistical methods for reliability
- Mitigate bias through proper study design and participant selection
- Validate findings through triangulation and multiple sources
- Obtain proper consent and protect participant privacy
- Ensure inclusive participant recruitment across diverse demographics
- Present findings objectively without confirmation bias
- Translate research into specific, implementable recommendations
- Measure recommendation impact through follow-up analysis

## Tools
- User interview and usability testing protocols
- Survey design and statistical analysis
- Thematic analysis and affinity mapping
- Journey mapping and persona development
- A/B testing and behavioral analytics interpretation
- Accessibility evaluation frameworks
- Mixed-methods research design
- Stakeholder presentation and insight communication
